What each service includes

Prepress

DTP & Prepress

  • Graphic design, typesetting, layout, color separation, film processing. Full prepress services for offset or publishing — copy preparation and page layout.
  • Professional image processing (retouch, preflight, ICC profiling, PDF/X, bleed, overprint).
  • Color separations and film / CTP production for offset.
Secure

Secure printing

Relief, planographic and intaglio printing combined with anti-counterfeit features for secure documents and value products.

What we print securely
  • Security print (shares, deposit certificates, diplomas, graduation certificates)
  • Stamps in 1–6 colors, on gummed or self-adhesive paper, various formats (diamond, rectangular)
  • Postal stationery, themed envelopes with prepaid stamp
  • Serialized labels / with edge perforation
Available security features
  • Paper with in-paper “FT” watermark
  • Paper with random fibers visible under UV light
  • Microtext, guilloché lines, anti-scan backgrounds
  • Holograms, blind emboss, perforation
  • Special inks: thermochromic, photochromic, iridescent and UV
  • Unique numbering, bar/QR codes and automatic series inserting
Traceability & control
  • Full traceability by series and batches, production register
  • In-process quality control and internal audit
  • Design consultancy to discreetly integrate anti-counterfeit elements
Philately

Stamps & philately

We produce complete philatelic issues — from concept and layout to printing, perforation, finishing and packing.

  • Stamps in 1–6 colors, in sheets/mini-sheets, various formats (diamond, rectangular)
  • Gummed or self-adhesive paper, philatelic adhesives/glues
  • Philatelic finishes: standard & special perforation, blind emboss, hot foil
  • Associated products: maximum cards, postcards (classic & semi-illustrated)
  • Postal stationery and themed envelopes / with prepaid stamp
  • Numbered series, inserting and complete sets for collectors
  • Premium packaging, folders/cases for presentation and collection
Digital & VDP

Digital print & VDP (variable data)

Fast personalization, photo-like quality and stable process color — ideal for short/medium runs and one-to-one campaigns, with direct prepress integration.

  • Full VDP: names, unique series, bar/QR codes, addresses, variable images & colors
  • Short/urgent runs with ICC profiling, PDF/X compliance and approved soft proof
  • Print substrates: 90–350 gsm papers, board, self-adhesive; SRA3+/banner formats
  • Inline/offline finishing: cutting, creasing, folding, stitching, perforation, lamination
  • Auto inserting & enveloping line (DL/C5/C4) with series verification
  • Batch traceability & QC reports; unique numbering & audit
  • Preflight & file prep, quick fixes and database integration (CSV/Excel), GDPR-compliant
Finishing

Finishing & binding

From folding and binding to lamination, varnishing, die-cutting and special effects — the finish gives your work its premium look.

Binding operations
  • Folding / creasing (leaflets, special folds)
  • Binding (perfect-bound, sewn, glued), saddle-stitch
  • Wire/coil binding (metal / plastic) — planners, catalogues, calendars
  • Rounded corners, trim to size, perforation
  • Die-cutting (special shapes, windows in folders/envelopes)
Protection & premium look
  • Lamination: gloss / matte / soft-touch
  • Total or spot varnish (UV spot / raised)
  • Hot foil (gold, silver, specials), blind emboss & deboss
Finished product examples
  • Presentation folders A4 / B4 — simple or with flaps, die-cut, varnished/laminated
  • Calendars: wall, triptych, desk, pocket (stapled / spiral)
  • Magazines (short/medium runs) — A5, A4, B5, B4, saddle-stitched or perfect-bound
  • Books (short runs) — perfect-bound or hardbound, A5, B5, A4, B4
  • Posters — up to 50 × 70 cm final size, optional varnish
  • Labels — serialized, edge-perforated (gummed or self-adhesive)
  • Personalized envelopes — die-cut window(s), prepaid stamp
Consumables

Consumables & other services

Related services and materials for complete workflows, maintenance and post-production.

  • Polymers for stamps (plate making), philatelic glue
  • Plate copy / CTP, development and dedicated chemistry
  • Guillotine blade sharpening, trimming setup & maintenance
  • Lamination foils & consumables (gloss / matte / soft-touch)
  • Paper & board-based materials: gummed / self-adhesive paper, blank labels
  • Packaging & logistics: folders/cases, DL/C5/C4 envelopes, presentation accessories
  • Scanning & archiving (electronic / physical) — on request

Note: we deliver standard consumables from stock and custom solutions on order for special runs or projects.
