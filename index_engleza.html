Why us?

Printing expertise for secure and memorable stamps

Anti-counterfeit security

Microtext, “FT” watermark, special inks (UV, thermo/photochromic), holograms and unique numbering — a complete protection suite for official issues.

3 printing technologies

We operate relief, planographic and intaglio — we choose the optimal technology for run size, substrate and required detail level.

Customization & DTP

Graphic concept, prepress and print proofs. Premium finishes: foil, embossing, blind emboss, perforation, gummed or self-adhesive glue.

Fast turnaround & QC

Clear planning, lot traceability and quality control at every step — on-time delivery for small and medium runs.

Official statements

On the modernization of the Romanian Post and the Stamp Factory

Sebastian Burduja

Minister of Energy
“The Post is modernizing — an important message showing how vital leadership stability is. We’re talking about a team that has performed even without a full mandate. I very much want us to have stability and predictability. The difference between a politician and a leader is simple: politicians think about the next elections, leaders think about the next generations. What we see today, here, is a team and a company thinking about future generations, looking at and respecting the past — a 160-year history — while also having a vision for the future.”

Valentin Ștefan

Director General, Romanian Post
“In the year when the Stamp Factory celebrates 150 years of activity and the Romanian Post marks 160 years, we are seeing many results of the company’s transformation. The equipment brought by the Romanian Post is the key to the Stamp Factory’s development in the medium and long term. It’s another step towards what we want to achieve at the Romanian Post: digitization, revitalization and performance. Simply put, from today, the Romanian Post can take part in major tenders and contracts without having to be a subcontractor or partner, being able to take on integrated work of printing, inserting and dispatch.”
